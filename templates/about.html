About Me

My name is Erik Hazzard and I'm the Princpal Software Engineer at Visual.ly. I spend the majority of my time developing javascript (client and server side) and python code, and figuring out how to best tell stories with data.

I'm also the author of OpenLayers Book: OpenLayers 2.10 Beginner's Guide , a book on making web maps and web development.

Previously, I was the Lead Web Developer for FREAC (Florida Resources and Environmental Analysis Center), a GIS shop based in Tallahassee, Florida.
My passions include game development, web development, open source software, and making knowledge easily accessible. I also enjoy writing, reading fantasy books, eating sunflower seeds.

I write about programming, OpenLayers, game development, and other tech-related things at my blog.

My latest projects, tinkerings, and things I'm working on can be found on the dev page or at my github page.
